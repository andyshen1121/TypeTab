## Name

## Short Description (132 chars max)
Quick tab search and smart duplicate tab detection. Find any tab instantly with Spotlight-style search.

## Detailed Description

Managing dozens of open tabs? TypeTab helps you stay organized.

**Spotlight Search**
Press Ctrl+Shift+K (Cmd+Shift+K on Mac) to instantly search all open tabs by title or URL. Navigate with arrow keys, press Enter to switch. Works across all windows.

**Duplicate Tab Detection**
When you open a page that's already open in another tab, TypeTab catches it immediately:
- Prompt mode: shows a notification asking if you want to switch to the existing tab
- Silent mode: automatically switches to the existing tab — the duplicate never loads

**Settings at a Click**
Click the TypeTab icon in the toolbar to access both Search and Settings:
- Enable/disable duplicate detection
- Choose prompt or silent mode
- URL-exact or domain-level matching

**Privacy First**
TypeTab works entirely locally. No data is collected, no external servers contacted. All tab information stays in your browser.

**Keyboard-Driven**
Designed for keyboard-first workflows. One shortcut to search, arrow keys to navigate, Enter to switch, Esc to close.
